# Feature Roadmap

This roadmap outlines planned features for the NHL Stat Tracker project.

## Phase 1: Advanced Analytics
- Implementation of xG (Expected Goals) tracking.
- Integration of possession metrics (Corsi and Fenwick).
- Development of zone entry and transition data visualizations.

## Phase 2: Historical Context
- Historical cap efficiency comparisons against champion rosters.
- Long-term draft performance analysis.
- Roster evolution timelines.

## Phase 3: Individual Player Data
- Interactive shot mapping.
- Contract efficiency scoring based on production vs. cap hit.
- Injury impact tracking.

## Phase 4: GM Simulation Tools
- Trade simulation engine with immediate cap impact.
- Five-year draft capital visualization.
- Buyout cost and penalty calculator.
